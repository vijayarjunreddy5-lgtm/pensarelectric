About Pensar Electric

Established in 2015, we've become a vital player in India's electrical engineering industry

PENSAR ELECTRIC PVT LTD., was established in 2015 and now plays a vital role in the Indian Electrical Engineering Industry.

Pensar is engaged in design, manufacture, testing and supply of HIGH VOLTAGE ISOLATORS from 11 kV to 420 kV, with current rating up to 4000 Amps, which are required by POWER SECTOR for generation, transmission and distribution of power.

We have successfully supplied our products to various State Electricity Board projects and Private Sector projects, establishing ourselves as a trusted partner in India's power infrastructure development.
